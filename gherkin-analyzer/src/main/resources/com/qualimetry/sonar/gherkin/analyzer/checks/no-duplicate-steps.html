Steps within a scenario or background must not be duplicated. Duplicate steps add no value and make scenarios harder to read and maintain. If the same step appears multiple times consecutively, it should be removed. If steps are repeated with different contexts, they may need to be consolidated or the scenario may need to be restructured.

Noncompliant Code Example

Feature: Shopping cart management

  Scenario: Add multiple items
    Given the customer is logged in
    When the customer adds "Laptop" to the cart
    When the customer adds "Laptop" to the cart    <!-- Noncompliant: duplicate -->
    Then the cart contains 2 items

  Background:                                     <!-- Noncompliant: duplicate in Background -->
    Given the customer is logged in
    Given the customer is logged in
    And the product catalog is available

Compliant Solution

Remove duplicate steps or consolidate them appropriately:

Feature: Shopping cart management

  Background:
    Given the customer is logged in
    And the product catalog is available

  Scenario: Add multiple items
    When the customer adds "Laptop" to the cart
    And the customer adds another "Laptop" to the cart
    Then the cart contains 2 items
